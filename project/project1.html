DESIGN RALLY 2025

Concept Project · UI/Visual Design

Project Background

This project explores a bold visual identity for a fictional design conference, “Design Rally 2025.” The goal was to experiment with energetic color palettes, dynamic type, and layout hierarchy.

Problem Statement

Most conference branding is corporate and predictable. This concept aimed to break that mold—appealing to young creatives while keeping content clear and accessible.

My Role

Visual Designer — responsible for concept development, layout, and presentation design.

Tools Used

Figma, Illustrator, Photoshop

Key Considerations

Strong hierarchy ensures titles, dates, and themes are instantly readable. High-contrast colors, bold type, and balanced decorative elements maintain clarity and accessibility across mediums.

Visuals

Next Steps

Expand the system into motion graphics, signage, and a responsive landing page to test flexibility across formats.

Reflection

Through this project, I explored how type and color can shape emotion and energy in an event setting. I learned to balance playfulness with readability, and I enjoyed pushing boundaries without real client constraints. It reminded me that strong visual identity doesn't need to sacrifice clarity or function.
